Our Services

Unwanted or faulty electrical & electronic waste?

Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE), is a constant battle for businesses – large or small. It includes most things that have a plug or need a battery. As a business you are duty-bound to finance the cost of the collection, treatment, recovery and environmentally-friendly disposal of new and old electrical and electronic waste.

Confidential laptop, computer or hard drive destruction

iWaste provide a number of solutions to guarantee that your data is securely eradicated, through thorough destruction practices. We can permanently destroy all residual data from your hard drives, compliant to government and industry standards. We have a wide range of clients including police, high profile individuals, government officials and large and small organisations.

Document Shredding Services at iWaste

We take information security very seriously and ensure that secure document shredding is carried out with exceptional care. We can provide mobile secure document shredding services at your premises, or off-site shredding services at our secure facilities. Schedule in your collections, as and when required and we can do the rest. We maintain excellent environmental credentials, with 100% of paper waste being recycled.

Intelligent Waste Management.

iWaste’s drive to provide flawless client services, in combination with the knowledge and experience possessed by the team, has helped iWaste gain a reputation as one of the UK’s most trusted E waste companies. We provide electronic waste collection services, secure data destruction, electronic recycling equipment and WEEE disposal from Hampshire, for businesses across the UK. Our client portfolio includes corporations such as BP Oil, 3M and public sector organisations such as the NHS.
